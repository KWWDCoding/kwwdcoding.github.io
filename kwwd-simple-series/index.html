Simple Series

A powerful WordPress plugin for organizing related content into beautiful, displayable series. Perfect for tutorials, courses, multi-part articles, and more.

Download Latest View on GitHub

Everything You Need to Organize Content

Simple Series gives you full control over how your related content is grouped, styled, and displayed.

📚

Unlimited Series

Create as many series as you need using the dedicated Series post type. Organize tutorials, courses, blog series, or any content collection.

🔗

Multi-Post Support

Assign posts to multiple series. A post about "Spider-Man" can belong to both a "Marvel" series AND a "Superhero" series.

⇅

Drag-to-Reorder

Easily reorder posts within a series with intuitive drag-and-drop. Keep your content in the right order.

🎨

Customizable Styling

Full control over appearance: background color, text color, font size, borders, padding, and list styles (bullets, numbers, roman numerals, and more).

📦

Collapsible Display

Series can start collapsed with a toggle, keeping your pages clean. Visitors can expand to see the full list when needed.

⚡

Shortcode Support

Display any series anywhere using `[simple_series id=X]`. Include multiple series on a single page if needed.
